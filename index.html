Stoned in Gielinor II

Album out July 5th on Dungeons Deep Records

Private LP stream and download

Stoned in Gielinor II is the sophomore collection of Dungeon Synth from Somerset’s foremost sorcerer, Flickers from the Fen. Concocted in the county’s deepest moor, the LP sets treasured memories of late night RPG sessions to music.

Invisible Oranges called FftF’s 2023 debut - Stoned in Gielinor I - a collection of "idyllic lo-fi melodies that promote warm escapism”, and the LP featured on end-of-year lists from Toilet Ov Hell, Sounds of the Void and Transmissions from the Dark.

Following a raucous live debut at Crypt of the Wizard and NTS Radio’s 'Life After Death Fest', Stoned in Gielinor II finds FftF augmenting their debut LP’s pastoral riffs and homely synths with soaring live strings to create a potent MIDIeval atmosphere. Steeped in classic Ambient Anglicana, the album maps a tapestry of fantasy nostalgia.

‘Morning in Catherby’ is the first choice cut. Kitsch MIDI and strings jostle for attention, placing the listener in the heart of a medieval square on market day. ‘Scuzz and Flame’ finds the record at its grandest, as brittle harpsichord scores a cathedral rave. Finally we have ‘For Guthix (Charge!!)’, a raucous last stand of dungeon drums, where the LP’s archaic melodies meet the electric horizon.
